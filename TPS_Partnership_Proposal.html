CosmoNFT Partnership Proposal

The Planetary Society Strategic Partnership

Executive Summary

CosmoNFT is a digital collectibles platform offering NFTs of celestial objects—stars, exoplanets, galaxies, and nebulae—with a mission to fund space exploration and education.

We propose a strategic partnership with The Planetary Society (TPS) wherein 30% of all primary sales revenue and secondary market royalties will be donated directly to TPS to support its mission of advancing space science and exploration.

This partnership represents a unique opportunity for TPS to engage a new generation of space enthusiasts while generating sustainable, recurring revenue through an innovative digital platform.

1. Project Overview

1.1 What is CosmoNFT?

CosmoNFT is a curated marketplace for digital collectibles representing real celestial objects. Each NFT is:

  • Scientifically Grounded: Based on actual astronomical data and discoveries
  • Uniquely Scored: Rated on a proprietary "Cosmic Score" system (0-500 points)
  • Dynamically Priced: Prices increase 7.5% weekly across 81 pricing phases
  • Blockchain Verified: Minted on the Polygon network for low fees and environmental sustainability

1.2 The Cosmic Score System

Each celestial object is evaluated across five categories (0-100 points each):

CategoryDescription
Fame & VisibilityPublic recognition and naked-eye visibility
Scientific SignificanceResearch importance and discoveries enabled
RarityUniqueness of the object type or characteristics
Discovery RecencyHow recently the object was discovered/studied

Badge Tiers:

  • ELITE (425-500): Exceptional celestial objects
  • PREMIUM (400-424): Highly significant objects
  • EXCEPTIONAL (375-399): Notable discoveries
  • STANDARD (350-374): Fascinating cosmic objects

4. Content & Curation

4.1 NFT Categories

Stars:

  • Famous stars (Sirius, Betelgeuse, Polaris)
  • Variable stars and binary systems
  • Historically significant stars

  • Habitable zone planets
  • Notable Kepler/TESS discoveries
  • Potentially Earth-like worlds

  • Supernova remnants

  • Pulsars and neutron stars
  • Asteroids and comets

6. Legal & Compliance

6.1 Structure

  • CosmoNFT operates as a separate legal entity
  • Formal donation agreement with TPS
  • Quarterly revenue reporting and transfers
  • Annual third-party audit of revenue sharing

6.2 Regulatory Considerations

  • NFTs structured as collectibles, not securities
  • No promises of financial returns
  • Clear terms of service
  • Geographic restrictions where required

6.3 Intellectual Property

  • Original artwork created for each NFT
  • Celestial objects are public domain
  • TPS logo usage per brand guidelines
  • Buyer receives collectible, not IP rights
